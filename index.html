The Challenge

Systemic Hospital Congestion & Stockouts

District hospitals suffer from acute supply constraints, inventory leaks, and sudden epidemics. Communication between Community Health Centres (CHCs) and state drug depots is slow, leading to critical stockouts when outbreaks strike.

The Breakthrough

Real-Time AI Resource Routing

Prahari acts as an automated neural coordinator. It ingests patient charts via voice or OCR prescription uploads, predicts district infection timelines 7-14 days in advance, and optimizes inventory routing maps automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Prahari protect patient privacy?

Prahari operates on fully secure FHIR data standards. All personally identifiable information (PII) is securely stripped on our server nodes before processing under strict encryption compliance guidelines.

Does it integrate with existing Government of India registries?

Yes, Prahari is built with plug-and-play APIs mapped against ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) guidelines and e-Aushadhi registers for seamless deployment across rural health centers.

What is the accuracy rate of outbreak predictions?

Using grounded Google search indices, local symptom patterns, and seasonal parameters, Prahari maintains an verified 91.4% confidence score for localized vector-borne epidemic spikes.
